Baroque(1600-1750)

Baroque is a genre of music popular between 1600 and 1750. Famous artists from this period include Johann Sebastian Bach, Antonio Vivaldi and Henry Purcell.

Three important characteristics distinguish Baroque from its contemporaries; a focus on upper and lower tones, layered melodies and an increase in orchestra sizes.

    Key instruments used:
  • Contrabasson
  • Bass clarinet
  • Piccolo
  • Xylophone
  • Drums
  • Celestes
  • Harps
  • Bells
  • Triangles
