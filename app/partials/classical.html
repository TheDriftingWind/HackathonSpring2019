Classical Music

Classical music refers to music created during the period of time between 1750 - 1830. The main instrument of this era was the piano as it can produce sounds with varying levels of volume. Classical music emphasized order and contains clear divisions between sections of music.

Famous Composers:

  • Ludwig van Beethoven
  • Johann Sebastian Bach
  • Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
  • Johannes Brahms
  • Richard Wagner
  • Claude Debussy
  • Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky
  • Joseph Haydn
  • Antonio Vivaldi
